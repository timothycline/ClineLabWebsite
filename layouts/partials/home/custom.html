Fisheries Ecology at MSU

Cline Lab — Department of Ecology, Montana State University

We study the ecology and management of cold-water fishes — particularly salmonids — in the face of climate change, habitat fragmentation, and shifting land use. Our work combines long-term field studies, physiological experiments, and quantitative modeling to answer questions that matter for conservation and sustainable fisheries.
