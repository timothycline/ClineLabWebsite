We genotyped [N] individuals across [X] tributary networks using a RADseq approach and applied circuit-theoretic resistance modeling to identify landscape features governing gene flow. Contrary to morphological evidence of isolation, we detected low but significant connectivity between [specific populations], implicating [feature] as a conduit for rare dispersal events.
