Congratulations to Ben Weber for presenting his research at the Crawfords Scholar Showcase! Ben presented a poster on *Assessing the Translocation of Bull Trout in Glacier National Park*, examining how introduced Yellowstone Cutthroat Trout have affected Bull Trout populations in Grace Lake and evaluating the effectiveness of translocation monitoring as a conservation strategy. Great work representing the lab, Ben!
