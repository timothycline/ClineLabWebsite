Congratulations to Lukas Draugelis on successfully defending his Master's thesis, *Rainbow and Hybrid Trout Production Dynamics in the Flathead River, MT*! Lukas's MS work identified sources of hybrid trout production in the Flathead River system, developing novel isotopic and genetic tools to disentangle the origins and demographics of rainbow × cutthroat hybrids — work with direct implications for native species conservation and invasive species management. Lukas will be staying in the lab to continue as a PhD student, with his dissertation focusing on life-history diversity at the leading edge of the rainbow trout invasion. Well done, Lukas!
