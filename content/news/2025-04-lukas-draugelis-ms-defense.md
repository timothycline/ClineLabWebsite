Congratulations to Lukas Draugelis on successfully defending his Master's thesis, *Rainbow and Hybrid Trout Production Dynamics in the Flathead River, MT*! Lukas's work examined how life-history strategies vary across the rainbow trout invasion front in the Flathead River system, providing key insights into the ecological mechanisms driving invasion success and informing ongoing suppression efforts to protect native bull trout and westslope cutthroat trout. Well done, Lukas — it has been a pleasure having you in the lab!
